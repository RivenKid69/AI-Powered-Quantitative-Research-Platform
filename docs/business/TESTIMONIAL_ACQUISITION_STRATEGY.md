### 3.4 Tier 3: Video Testimonials

**Format:** 2-5 minute video
**Use Case:** Website, YouTube, sales meetings, conferences

**Script Framework:**

1. **Introduction (15 sec):** Name, role, company overview
2. **Challenge (30 sec):** What problem were you trying to solve?
3. **Solution (45 sec):** How did QuantBot help?
4. **Results (45 sec):** What outcomes did you achieve?
5. **Recommendation (15 sec):** Who would you recommend this to?

**Production Guidelines:**

- Remote recording via Zoom/Teams (reduce friction)
- Offer professional editing as incentive
- Provide talking points, not a script (authenticity)
- 1080p minimum, good lighting/audio
- 3-take maximum (respect time)

## 4. Logo Acquisition Strategy

### 4.1 Permission Levels

| Level | Description | Use Case |
|-------|-------------|----------|
| **Level 1** | Anonymous ("A European prop firm") | Case studies, metrics |
| **Level 2** | Named but no logo | Written testimonials |
| **Level 3** | Logo for specific use | Website "trusted by" |
| **Level 4** | Full public reference | Press releases, marketing |

### 4.2 Acquisition Approach

**Step 1: Ask Early (During Pilot Signup)**

Include in pilot agreement:
> "With your permission, we may reference [Company Name] as a pilot participant.
> Please indicate your preference:
> - [ ] Yes, you may use our company name publicly
> - [ ] Yes, but only in private sales contexts
> - [ ] No, please keep our participation confidential"

**Step 2: Re-Ask After Success**

Customers who initially declined may agree after positive experience.

**Step 3: Offer Value Exchange**

| Customer Concern | Response/Offer |
|------------------|----------------|
| "Competitors will know" | Anonymous industry category only |
| "Legal approval required" | We'll work with your legal team |
| "What do we get?" | Co-marketing, discount, early access |
| "Too much exposure" | Limited use (website only, no press) |

### 4.3 Logo Display Best Practices

- **Grayscale logos** for uniformity (unless premium tier)
- **Minimum 5 logos** before displaying section (credibility threshold)
- **Refresh quarterly** with new additions
- **Permission documentation** stored for legal compliance

## 10. Legal & Compliance

### 10.1 Testimonial Release Form

All testimonials require signed release covering:
- Permission to use name/company/logo
- Confirmation testimonial is truthful
- Right to edit for length/clarity
- Duration of use (typically indefinite)
- Revocation process

### 10.2 Regulatory Considerations (Financial Services)

**EU/MiCA Context:**
- No performance guarantees implied
- Past performance disclaimers where needed
- No misleading claims

**Standard Disclaimer:**

> "Customer testimonials reflect individual experiences and may not
> be typical. Past performance is not indicative of future results.
> [QuantBot] does not guarantee any specific investment outcomes."
